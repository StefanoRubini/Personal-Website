UNLV Hixson-Lied Success Scholars Scholarship Program

  • The Hixson-Lied Success Scholars program is a prestigious scholarship program awarded to ~25 students enrolled at UNLV, which offered $1,250 per semester ($2,500 per academic year, $10,000 total) for every recipient. The program duration is 8 semesters, in which students are required to maintain full-time enrollment at UNLV every semester, maintain a 3.0 semester GPA, complete four hours of service weekly, serve as peer leaders to other UNLV undergraduate students/future scholars, etc.
